date: 2025-03-15
task_type: funnel_analysis
keywords: funnel, drop-off, mobile, checkout
triggered_by: "why is mobile checkout 40% lower than desktop"

## Situation
User asked for funnel comparison mobile vs desktop.
Initial analysis showed 40pp gap. Stakes: medium (product decision).

## Approach used
1. Loaded EXPERIENCE_INDEX → matched funnel,drop-off
2. Loaded ep_009 (similar, older) → noted traffic split was missing
3. Applied traffic-split-first rule before computing funnel
4. Found: gap mostly explained by different traffic source (paid vs organic)
5. Delivered with explicit "this is correlation, not product UX issue" caveat

## Outcome
recommendation_adopted: yes
stakeholder_feedback: "this reframing saved us from a bad redesign decision"
quality_self_score: 4/5 (took longer than expected due to traffic split step)

## What to reuse
- Always check traffic source composition before funnel comparison
- Lead with "gap is explained by X" framing, not "mobile is worse"

## What to avoid
- Don't present raw funnel without traffic normalization
